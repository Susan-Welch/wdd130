Overview

Purpose

In order to increase revenue, this website will advertise different packages the company offers and answer the frequently asked questions extremeists want to know when booking their next adventure. The quality of the site will allow customers to feel comfortable hiring this business even before they've contacted the company. With a fresh, excited energy in the style of this website, customers will be enticed to book a trip with this business over the competition. As a result of having a fresh, professial, exciting website, revenue will increase and the business will thrive.

Audience

Adventure seekers look no further! Vacationers aged 25-35 who are looking to have an extreme experience are the target audience for this site. These thrill seekers are young professionals who like to spend money on making terrific memories. They also love to have a great story to post on social media. These young, active people need to feel good about the extremity of the adventure and also see the quality and professionalism of the business. Because of the age range of the target audience, the website must look fantastic on mobile devices as well as PC's.
